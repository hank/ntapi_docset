NtQueryPerformanceCounter
NtQueryPerformanceCounter

NTSYSAPI 
NTSTATUS
NTAPI


NtQueryPerformanceCounter(



  OUT PLARGE_INTEGER      PerformanceCounter,
  OUT PLARGE_INTEGER      PerformanceFrequency OPTIONAL );



  • PerformanceCounter Result is number of processor ticks after last reset.
  • PerformanceFrequency It's number of processor ticks per one second.


Another method of uptime calculation :

UpTime = PerformanceCounter / PerformanceFrequency;
